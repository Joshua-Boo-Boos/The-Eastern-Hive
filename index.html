Server Rules

# Rule Description
1 No Cheating Use of hacks, exploits, or third-party software is strictly prohibited.
2 Respect All Players Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, racism, or hate speech.
3 Follow Commander Orders Listen to your commander and work as a team to achieve victory.
4 No Team Stacking Keep teams balanced. Auto-team balance must be respected.
5 No Griefing Do not intentionally damage team structures or block teammates.
6 Communicate Use voice chat and text chat constructively. Call out enemy positions.
7 No Spawn Camping Give the opposing team a fair chance. Excessive spawn camping is not allowed.
8 Have Fun! Remember, it's a game. Enjoy the experience and make friends!
